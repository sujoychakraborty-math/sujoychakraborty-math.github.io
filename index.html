Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a DST-INSPIRE faculty fellow at the department of Mathematics, IISER Tirupati. Prior to this, I was an Institute Post-Doctoral fellow at the department of Mathematics, IISER Tirupati and IIT Madras. I obtained my Ph.D in Mathematics from TIFR Mumbai under the guidance of Prof. Indranil Biswas.
